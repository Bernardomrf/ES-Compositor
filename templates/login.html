How does Transafe works?

  • Transafe sits in the middle of your transactions, between you and the seller.
  • When you buy something you pay us and the money is not immediatly sent to the seller
  • The seller is now assured of his money and then dispatches the item providing the tracking number.
  • When you receive the package you go to Transafe to confirm everything went well and provide the seller with feedback, at this point the seller receives the money for the product.
